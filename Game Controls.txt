-- SHIP--

- W/S to raise and lower the sails
- Q/E to turn the main sail left and right
- A/D to turn the wheel left and right
- Space to raise and lower the anchor

-- CANNONS -- 

- Hold right click to aim the cannons
- Left click to first the cannon

-- CAMERA --

- Scroll wheel to zoom in and out
- Escape to unlock the mouse cursor (left click to lock again)
